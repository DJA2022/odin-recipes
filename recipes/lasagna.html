Beef Lasagna Recipe

Beef Lasagna with rich tomatoe sauce

This lasagna is made from fresh beef mince, roma tomatoes and creamly white sauce

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Tomatoe sauce
  • Beef mince
  • Garlic
  1. Make white sauce
  2. Make beef mince sauce
  3. Prepare pasta sheets
  4. Layer items and cover with cheese
  5. Bake in oven at 180deg for 30 minutes
